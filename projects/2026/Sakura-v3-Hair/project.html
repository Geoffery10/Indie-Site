Sakura finally has new hair!


This new hair model has been a long time coming. Sakura has been around since 2021 and her original hair was a Vroid hair I found on booth. That defined her character for around 3 years until I reworked it in 2024 with the v2 Hair.

While the v2 hair was completely original it really suffered from my lack of experience modeling hair. It was very tentacle like and didn't quite match my vision for Sakura. It also had plenty of technical issues like bad topology, poor UVs, and no physics whatsoever. Upon it's completion there was already a desire to create a v3 that would much better match my vision for Sakura.

Finally in 2026, I completed the v3 hair model. Taking some of the extra free time I had from being laid off, I was able to devote a significant amount of time to rebuilding the hair from scratch. This new hair is much more in line with my vision for Sakura and has a much more natural look. It also has better topology, UVs, and physics. I'm really happy with how it turned out and I think it really brings Sakura's character to life.
